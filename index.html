About League Of Legends

League of Legends

is a team-based strategy game where two teams of five powerful champions face off to destroy the other’s base.Choose from over 140 champions to make epic plays, secure kills,and take down towers as you battle your way to victory

Lanes In League Of Legends

There are 3 lanes in League of Legends such as Top lane, Middle Lane

or Mid lane, and Bottom lane. These 3 lanes are connected by Jungle area.

Top Lane

Top lane is recommmended for Fighter, Assassin, or Tank champion to play in. It is a solo (1v1) lane.

Mid Lane

Mid lane is recommended for Mage or Assassin champion to play in. It is also a solo (1v1) lane.

Jungle

Jungle is not specify as a lane but as an area that has camp with monster for Jungler to play in.

Bottom Lane

Bottom lane is designated for Marksman and Support champion to play in. It is a duo 2v2 lane.

League of Legends Champion Categories

League of Legends has six different categories for champions

Assassins

Move quickly and deal high amounts of damage to a single target. These champions are best used for quick fights and jumping out to safety.

Fighters/Bruisers

These champions can deal and take a lot of damage. Fighters are excellent in extended battles.

Mages

These champions are best used in distance scenarios. If mages are well protected, they can deal a lot of damage while being invulnerable to attacks.

Marksmen

Just like mages, marksmen are best utilized when damage is dealt at a distance.

Support

The main role of support characters are to amplify their teammate’s strengths and abilities.

Tank

Similar to fighters, tanks are best implemented as a “damage sponge.”
